Module 7 - Unit 12

Ethics in the Digital Landscape



This week’s learning explores concerns related to Ethics in the digital landscape. You will consider issues affecting legality of state’s actions as well as individuals’. The learning will also look at ethics and challenges to police practice, and issues concerning responsiveness to unknown future technologies. You will also consider the issues concerning the legalities and illegalities of hacking.


On completion of this unit you will be able to:
- Explore issues concerning ethics in the cyberspace.
- Practice critical thinking skills.
- Practice research skills.
