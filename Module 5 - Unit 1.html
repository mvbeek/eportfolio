Module 5 - Unit 1

Human Factors and Cyber Security


LECTURECAST SUMMARY

- Humans form an integral part of information systems.

- Human behaviour is a major source of concern for cyber security.

- While some factors can be attributed to malicious individuals inside the organisation, others can be attributed   to unintended threats.

- Some of these human factors can be addressed at both individual and organisational levels and these will be   explored in subsequent lecturecast.


READING

The reading this week focuses on understanding the causes of security breaches caused by human factors and their impact on the security profile of an organisation.

Your reading should begin with Johnson, J. (2021) for the introduction. Further reading will introduce the concept of usable security and their implications in designing security solutions. The additional reading will introduce the concept of ‘insider’ threat.
