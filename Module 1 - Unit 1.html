Module 1 - Unit 1

Launching into Cyber Security May 2021

Collaborative Learning Discussion 1

Discuss why Cyber Security is now a global issue and why it is important for companies to invest in Cyber Security.

INITIAL POST

Cyber security has become a global issue due to the speedy evolution of the internet. Despite expeditious growth, we still rely on legacy protocols and infrastructure which was designed decades ago. This rapid development has left gaps, which require patches, bug fixes, and workarounds thus creating vulnerabilities (Tofan, 2018). Everything is now connected through the internet and any information is widely available online, thus it needs protecting.
 
It is important for companies to invest in cyber security because the cybercrime culture has overtaken the technology of the cybersecurity field, everything in cyberspace is vulnerable and subject to risk (Spalevic, 2014). During July 2020 at the height of a global pandemic and with many people working remotely, Twitter experienced the largest privacy and security hack in history “through a mobile spear-phishing attack” (Samah, 2020). Hackers commandeered the “Twitter accounts of former President Barack Obama, presidential hopeful Joe Biden, Elon Musk, Jeff Bezos, Kim Kardashian, and Apple, among others” (Bizcommunity, 2020). The significance of a breach like this on the surface is dual fold, which could appear to be a high-profile financial scam but because social media plays a huge part in 21st-century society this type of attack has the potential to cause many more serious national and political implications. Imagine the implications of what would happen if President Doland trump’s Twitter account was hacked, and the hacker sent out a tweet relating to a North Korea nuclear attack (Bizcommunity, 2020). This attack damaged Twitter's reputation by highlighting concerns with social media corporations relating to tax disputes as well as anti-trust concerns (Oxford, 2020). In order to gain the trust of their subscribers, Twitter had to explain the root cause as well as what steps they would take to mitigate this in the future (Bizcommunity, 2020). 

Cyber security has become such a huge issue on a global scale that corporations are now being forced to structure the functionality of their infrastructure and operations around cyber threat analysis and mitigation protocols. This trend will only continue to develop strongly in the years ahead of us. The above serves to show how critical cybersecurity is on a global scale and how large firms investing in cyber security practices can help prevent such breaches of personal information. The weigh up to any corporation is the value of the protection provided, against the cost of the initial investment in implementing the mitigating security protocols.
