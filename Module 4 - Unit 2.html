Module 4 - Unit 2

UML Modelling to Support Secure System Planning 


In this unit you will gain some practical skills in the creation of UML flow charts. Flow charts are used during the design stage of the SDLC and can be used from that point onwards to support communications between stakeholders and to act as documentation of the final product created.

In this unit you shall:
Gain an awareness of the opportunities for integrating software security development practices at each stage of the agile SDLC.
Understand the range of ways in which software security can be problematic.

READING

The reading this week focusses on terminology specific to security management from the ISO/IEC, who are key standardisation bodies within the field. This terminology is further reinforced through the consideration of the application of security activities by Sharma and Bawa (2020).

Sharma, A. & Bawa, R. K. (2020) Identification and Integration of Security Activities for Secure Agile Development. International Journal of Information Technology.
