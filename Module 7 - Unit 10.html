READING

The reading this week focusses on issues concerning international and transnational cooperation in dealing with cyber-criminality. There is no required reading for this week. The following readings are recommended, and you will find them useful for adding context to your seminar preparation.

Required Reading

Henseler, H. & van Loenhout, S. (2018) Educating judges, prosecutors and lawyers in the use of digital forensic experts. Digital Investigation 24: 576-582
