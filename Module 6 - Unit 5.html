Module 6 - Unit 5

Current and Future Challenges of Operating Systems and Distributed Systems


This week we shall gain an appreciation of some of the current and potential future challenges of developing operating and distributed systems. This considers challenges such as the scale of operations, in addition to the variety of solutions which have been provisioned.


On completion of this unit you will be able to:
- Appreciate the challenges of running secure operating and distributed systems in the modern world.
- Research the state-of-the-art literature to examine current approaches used to support distributed networking.
- Research the use of system modelling tools.
